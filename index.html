Input Sources

DESKTOP PC Built-in classroom computer
MAC Built-in Apple computer (Mac Mini) already connected to the system
LAPTOP Use the HDMI or USB-C cable at the teaching station to connect your laptop
DOC CAM Document camera to show physical documents, books, or objects to students
CAMERA Room camera with saved presets. Use these to quickly switch between different angles
BLU-RAY Play movies or videos from Blu-ray discs. Control playback with on-screen menu
WIRELESS STREAM Share your screen wirelessly from your device without any cables

Screen Controls

PREVIEW Check what content looks like on your screen before students can see it
PROJECT Show your content on the projectors around the room
HIDE & MUTE Hide the projector screens and mute audio (when all screens are hidden, audio is also muted)
FREEZE Pause what students see while you prepare the next slide or page in private
PLAY AUDIO Send sound from your selected input through the classroom speakers
